FONTS FEATURES The M+ OUTLINE FONTS are distributed with proportional Latin (4 variations), fixed-halfwidth Latin (3 variations) and fixed-fullwidth Japanese (2 Kana variations) character set 7 weights from Thin to Black are included, but fixed-halfwidth Latin with 5 weights from Thin to Bold.
