I. Installation and Auto-Update.  The Software's \r\ninstallation and auto-update processes transmit a \r\nlimited amount of data to Sun (or its service \r\nprovider) about those specific processes to help Sun \r\nunderstand and optimize them.  Sun does not associate \r\nthe data with personally identifiable information.  \r\nYou can find more information about the data Sun \r\ncollects at http://java.com/data/.
